##### Teleport

When a creature [teleports](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Movement/Teleport), they move from one space to another space instantaneously. The following rules apply to [teleporting](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Movement/Teleport):

- [Teleporting](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Movement/Teleport) doesn't provoke opportunity attacks or other effects that are triggered by a creature moving.
- When a creature [teleports](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Movement/Teleport), they bypass any obstacles between the space they leave and their destination space.
- A creature [teleporting](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Movement/Teleport) themself must have line of effect to their destination space. A creature [teleporting](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Movement/Teleport) another creature must have line of effect from the space the [teleported](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Movement/Teleport) creature leaves and to their destination space.
- A [teleporting](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Movement/Teleport) creature's destination space can't be occupied by another creature or object.
- The effect that lets a creature [teleport](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Movement/Teleport) indicates how far they can [teleport](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Movement/Teleport). That distance can be greater than the creature's speed.
- If a creature can [teleport](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Movement/Teleport) as part of their usual movement, they can use the Advance move action to [teleport](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Movement/Teleport) a number of squares up to their usual speed, unmodified by conditions or effects.
- If a creature [teleports](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Movement/Teleport) while [prone](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Conditions/Prone), they can be standing when they reach their destination space provided they are able to stand. If a [prone](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Conditions/Prone) creature is [teleported](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Movement/Teleport) by another creature, it is up to that creature whether the [teleported](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Movement/Teleport) creature remains [prone](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Conditions/Prone) or stands if they are able.
- If you [teleport](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Movement/Teleport) while affected by the [grabbed](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Conditions/Grabbed) or [restrained](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Conditions/Restrained) conditions, those conditions end for you.
- When a creature [teleports](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Movement/Teleport), they must leave the space where they start and enter a new space. A creature can't [teleport](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Movement/Teleport) to and from the same space.
