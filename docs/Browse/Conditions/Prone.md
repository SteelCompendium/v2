##### Prone

While a creature is [prone](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Conditions/Prone), they are flat on the ground, any strike they make takes a bane, and melee abilities used against them gain an edge. A [prone](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Conditions/Prone) creature must [crawl](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Movement/Crawl) to move along the ground, which costs 1 additional square of movement for every square crawled. A creature can't [climb](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Movement/Climb%20or%20Swim), [jump](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Movement/Jump), [swim](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Movement/Climb%20or%20Swim), or [fly](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Movement/Fly) while [prone](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Conditions/Prone). If they are [climbing](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Movement/Climb%20or%20Swim), [flying](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Movement/Fly), or [jumping](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Movement/Jump) when knocked [prone](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Conditions/Prone), they fall.

Unless the ability or effect that imposed the [prone](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Conditions/Prone) condition says otherwise, a [prone](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Conditions/Prone) creature can stand up using the Stand Up maneuver (see [Maneuvers](#page-287-0) in [Chapter 10: Combat](#page-288-0)). A creature adjacent to a willing [prone](https://steelcompendium.io/v2/Browse/Rules/Conditions/Prone) creature can likewise use the Stand Up maneuver to make that creature stand up.
