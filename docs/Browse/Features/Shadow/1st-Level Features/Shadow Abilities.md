#### Shadow Abilities

You specialize in dealing damage, then getting out of harm's way before the inevitable counterattack. You know a number of unique martial abilities that define your presence on the battlefield.

##### Signature Ability

Choose one signature ability from the following options. Signature abilities can be used at will. (*Quick Build:* [Teamwork Has Its Place](../../../../../Browse/Abilities/Shadow/1st-Level%20Features/Teamwork%20Has%20Its%20Place).)
